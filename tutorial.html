Geography Course Tutorial Page

Welcome to the geography course tutorial page!

Here, you will find a comprehensive collection of tutorials to help you master the course material. Each tutorial is designed to provide step-by-step guidance and examples to enhance your learning experience. Let's explore the tutorials available:

  1. Introduction to Geography



    In this tutorial, we will introduce the course objectives and provide an overview of the field of geography.

    Topics covered:

    • Definition and scope of geography
    • Branches of geography
    • Importance of geography in understanding the world

  • Physical Geography

    This module focuses on the physical aspects of geography, including landforms, climate, and ecosystems.

    • Tutorial 1: Landforms

      In this tutorial, we will explore various landforms found on Earth and their formation processes.

      Topics covered:

      • Mountains
      • Plateaus
      • Plains
      • Rivers and valleys

    • Tutorial 2: Climate

      In this tutorial, we will study different climate zones and factors influencing climate patterns.

      Topics covered:

      • Tropical climates
      • Temperate climates
      • Polar climates
      • Climate change

    • Tutorial 3: Ecosystems

      This tutorial focuses on understanding ecosystems and their ecological processes.

      Topics covered:

      • Terrestrial ecosystems
      • Aquatic ecosystems
      • Biodiversity
      • Human impacts on ecosystems

        Thematic map
        Climate map
        Road map
        Satellite map
        Historical map

      • GIS and spatial analysis

      This tutorial focuses on remote sensing technologies and their applications in geography.

      Topics covered:

      • Aerial photography

      Topics covered:
